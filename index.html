Welcome, defender

  • Your towers shape the road. Enemies pathfind around them — where you build is the maze. A line of palisades isn't a wall; it's a re-routing.
  • Four resources fund the war: 🪵 wood, 🪙 gold, 🪨 stone, 🌾 grain. Each tower costs a different mix. Watch the bar at the top-left.
  • The village is what you defend. Each villager who reaches the goal is killed by the breaching enemy — and your income decays with each loss. If they all fall, the realm falls.
  • Build at your own pace. Click a tower button at the bottom, then click a grass tile. When you're ready, press Start Wave.
